
						WORDS TO USE IN SENTENCES 

1. Dissent (n) - the holding or expression of opinions at variance with those commonly or officially held.

2. resemble (v) - have a similar appearance to or qualities in common with (someone or something); look or seem like.

3. stirring (adj) - causing excitement or strong emotion; rousing.

4. vivacious (adj) - (especially of a woman) attractively lively and animated.

5. inventive (adj) - having the ability to create or design new things or to think originally

6. twinkling (adj) - shining with a gleam that changes from bright to faint.

7. shrill (adj) - (of a voice or sound) high-pitched and piercing.

8. persuasive (adj) - good at persuading someone to do or believe something through reasoning or the use of temptation.

9. apathy (n) - lack of interest, enthusiasm, or concern.

10. counteract (v) - act against (something) in order to reduce its force or neutralize it.
